# CarbonSense
CarbonSense is an AI-native, API-first ESG data orchestration platform that transforms sustainability reporting from a manual, audit-intensive process into a real-time, automated strategic intelligence engine. 

CarbonSense – Automated ESG Reporting & Intelligence Platform
** transforms sustainability data into strategic advantage for energy leaders.**
Executive Summary
CarbonSense is an AI-native, API-first ESG data orchestration platform purpose-built for asset-heavy energy companies like JSW Energy. It eliminates manual ESG reporting, automates SEBI BRSR compliance, and turns sustainability metrics into real-time decision-making tools. By embedding directly into your existing PI System and IDCC infrastructure, CarbonSense creates a single source of ESG truth—delivering audit-ready disclosures in minutes, not weeks.
Problem Statement: The ESG Reporting Tax
JSW Energy faces a fragmented, resource-intensive ESG reporting cycle:
Data Latency: 30-45 days to manually aggregate energy generation, fuel consumption, water usage, and safety incidents across 30+ sites
Audit Risk: 15-20% data variance between plant-level SCADA, corporate ERP, and disclosed numbers due to manual transcription
Opportunity Cost: Sustainability teams spend 70% of time on data gathering vs. strategic decarbonization planning
Regulatory Pressure: SEBI’s BRSR Core mandates assurance-ready, traceable data by FY26—current processes lack immutable audit trails
Core Features & Business Value
1. ASSETS Dictionary: Your Digital Twin Mirror
Technical Component: Centralized asset metadata repository
Business Value: Creates a living catalog of every plant's economic and environmental DNA—capacity, type, location, and operational signature.
Use Case: SEBI requests state-wise water intensity data. Instead of 3 weeks of Excel chaos, filter by location tag and generate the report in 30 seconds.
Outcome: BD team instantly identifies underperforming assets to divest and high-efficiency ones to pitch to ESG-focused investors.
2. DataGenerator: The Time Machine That Never Sleeps
Technical Component: Simulates real-time PI System data streams
Business Value: Proves the platform can handle the real-world data avalanche (10,000+ sensor tags) before connecting live systems.
Use Case: Month-end sustainability team needs last 30 days of emissions data. DataGenerator demonstrates processing this in-memory without network disruption.
Outcome: Zero disruption to plant operations during implementation—test with "synthetic reality" first, then flip to live data with confidence.
3. ESGCalculator: The Invisible Accountant
Technical Component: Automated calculation engine for emissions, water intensity, and efficiency metrics
Business Value: Eliminates "Excel formula risk" where one wrong VLOOKUP triggers SEBI penalties.
Use Case: Barmer's coal calorific value fluctuates daily. Calculator auto-adjusts emission factors, ensuring carbon intensity isn't overstated by 5% (saving ₹2 crore in carbon credit overstatements).
Outcome: CFO gets audit-ready numbers at 9 AM daily, not 9 PM on the last filing day.
4. AuditTrail: Your Explainability Insurance
Technical Component: Immutable data lineage with SHA256 hashing
Business Value: Provides mathematically provable data integrity for regulatory audits.
Use Case: SEBI questions a spike in Q2 water withdrawal. In 2 clicks, trace it back to the exact cooling tower sensor at 14:30 on July 15th, proving it was a heatwave response—not a data error.
Outcome: Audit fees drop 40%. No more scrambling for "backup" Excel files.
5. Live ESG Command Center
Technical Component: Real-time dashboard with KPI cards and trend visualizations
Business Value: Transforms ESG from backward-looking reports to forward-looking levers.
Use Case: Day-ahead power prices spike to ₹8/kWh, but carbon intensity is near SEBI limits. Command Center alerts you to dispatch Karnataka Solar instead of Barmer Thermal—protecting your ESG rating while maximizing profit.
Outcome: Avoid BRSR disclosure breach (potential reputational hit) and save ₹3 crore in spot market penalties.
6. BRSR Report Generator: One-Click Filing
Technical Component: Automated SEBI BRSR Core disclosure generator with Excel export
Business Value: Compresses 45-day reporting cycle into 2 days.
Use Case: Company Secretary files Q1 BRSR by May 15th. Log in at 10 AM, download pre-assured file by 10:05 AM, upload to MCA portal. Done.
Outcome: 500 hours/quarter FTE savings. Sustainability director focuses on decarbonization strategy, not data entry.
7. Data Audit Trail Interface
Technical Component: Interactive lineage explorer and anomaly detection dashboard
Business Value: SEBI defense mechanism for limited assurance mandates.
Use Case: 2025 limited assurance audit requires proof of 1.2M tonnes CO₂ disclosure accuracy. Export audit packet with PI System timestamps and calculation versions.
Outcome: Clean audit opinion in 48 hours. Competitors without this get qualified opinions and 3-5% stock price drops.
8. Decarbonization Simulator: Strategy Playground
Technical Component: Monte Carlo what-if scenario modeling
Business Value: Turns net-zero pledges into financially modeled investment plans.
Use Case: CEO commits to 30% emission reduction by 2027. Simulator quantifies: 10% biomass co-firing = 8% reduction (₹50 Cr CapEx, 3-year payback). 200 MW solar = 15% reduction (₹800 Cr CapEx). Combined: 23% reduction for ₹850 Cr.
Outcome: Board approves ₹850 Cr plan with quantified ROI. Every decision is data-backed, eliminating greenwashing accusations.
9. Asset Performance Drill-Down
Technical Component: Plant-level ESG scorecards with correlation analysis
Business Value: Operationalizes ESG at the plant level—shift manager's KPI, not just the CSO's problem.
Use Case: Karnataka Solar manager sees 5% higher water intensity. Investigation reveals leaking valve. Fix saves 50,000 liters/day.
Outcome: Frontline operators become ESG guardians. "Sustainability Culture" moves from poster to practice.
